伍 · Questions

Before you cross

What kind of game is Badinoris?

Badinoris: Beyond The Gates is a hand-drawn 2D action-adventure with deliberate, souls-like combat and interconnected, metroidvania-style worlds. Battle, explore, and uncover the secrets of a broken spirit realm.

When does it release?

It's coming soon. An exact date hasn't been announced yet — follow the studio's channels to be notified the moment it launches.

Is there a demo?

Yes — a free demo is available now, so you can step through the gates before release. Just hit Download and pick a build.

What platforms is it on?

Windows 10 (64-bit) and macOS (Apple Silicon, M1 and newer). Full controller support is included.

How difficult is it?

It's a deliberate, souls-like challenge. Enemies and bosses each have their own abilities and patterns — study them, then strike. The difficulty is part of the journey.

Which languages are supported?

English, Spanish (Spain), Greek, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and Turkish.

Who is making it?

Badinoris is created by indie studio YIGIDO — a single-player adventure, hand-drawn with love.
